# GAMEPLAY DIFFICULTY




Gameplay difficulty adjusts certain factors such as zombie strength and rewards.
Depending on the difficulty, there are also additional changes.

Basic: Low Zombie health, damage, XP and cash rewards (80%, 75%, 85%, 85% respectively).
- Additional stat changes:
== Stamina Regen 108%
== Thirst, Hunger, Fatigue and Infection progress at 80% rate.
- Note: Best for new/low skilled players.

(Default)
Normal: Normal zombie health, damage and rewards.
- Note: Recommended for players who have learned how to play this gamemode in general.

Advanced: Increased Zombie health, damage, XP and cash rewards. (115%, 120%, 120%, 110% respectively)
- Note: Recommended for significantly skilled players.

Challenging: Higher Zombie health, damage, XP and cash rewards. (140%, 150%, 130%, 120% respectively)
- Additional stat changes:
== Stamina Regen 96%
- Note: Recommended for advanced players. New players probably won't survive this...

Ultimate: Greater Zombie health, damage, XP and cash rewards. (160%, 175%, 160%, 140%)
- Additional stat changes:
== Stamina Regen 93%
== Thirst, Hunger, Fatigue and Infection progress at 125% rate
- Note: Recommended for highly skilled players and veterans.

Hell: Harsh Zombie health and damage, but greatly increased XP and cash rewards. (200%, 225%, 200%, 175% respectively)
- Additional stat changes:
== Stamina Regen 88%
== Thirst, Hunger, Fatigue progress at 150% rate
== Infection progresses at 250% rate
== Carry weight 0.9x
== Infection also damages you by 1hp per second after it reaches 50%.
- Note: Only for those who have mastered the gameplay of this gamemode.

Impossible: Extremely high Zombie health and damage, but a lot more XP and cash rewards. (500%, 300%, 400%, 350% respectively)
- Additional stat changes:
== Stamina Regen 75%
== Thirst, Hunger, Fatigue progress at 250% rate
== Infection progresses at 666% rate
== Carry weight 0.75x
== Infection also damages you by 1hp per second after it reaches 10%, can reach up to 3 dps after reaching 50%..
- Note: Only for the best of the best! No one's able to survive this hell...





















































# ECONOMY DIFFICULTY




Economic difficulty spices up the game and strategic thinking by adjusting item buy/sell prices to match the difficulty it belongs to.
Depending on the economy difficulty, you will also gain more XP gain from killing zombies.
^ By default, this feature is enabled. If you are a server owner, you are freely able to disable this through code/server convar.

Economic difficulty list:
-# (#1: item buy price mul, #2: item sell price mul, #3: xp gain mul, respectively, in %)
- Easy: 85%, 110%, 95%
- Standard: 100%, 100%, 100% (default)
- Difficult: 120%, 85%, 105%
- Complex: 150%, 75%, 110%
- Harsh: 200%, 60%, 120%
- Hellish: 250%, 50%, 130%

































# INFLATION DIFFICULTY

RECOMMENDED FOR ADVANCED PLAYERS ONLY.
Inflation is a factor adding significant economic difficulty by dynamically increasing costs of item prices bought from/sold to traders

Increases the cost of items bought from traders and increases selling costs as well.
Cycles every 28 days, by default. (Counts the days passed after the value was initially setup on the server)
After the amount of days pass by around 60%, inflation starts to decrease until it hits the max days. Inflation starts at days passed by 5%.
Counts hours too. (For reference, an hour is 1/24 days)

Selection of inflation difficulties:
-# (max buy price, max sell price in %, respectively)
– None (Inflation disabled)
– Low (104%, 101%)
– Medium (108%, 102%)
– Normal (115%, 105%)
– Intermediate (125%, 108%)
– High (140%, 115%)
– Very High (155%, 120%)
– World Crisis (190%, 125%)

Note that this feature has a great impact on economic difficulty, depending on the settings.
